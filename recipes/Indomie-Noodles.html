Indomie's Recipe

Making instant noodles is pretty easy. You should have a pan for more flavor, but it's fine if you want to use a bot. The ingredients you need are included in the bag already. They are:

  • The noodles
  • The spices
  • The oil

The steps are very simple. Here's what you should cook:

  1. Get a Pot or a Pan (my personal option.)
  2. Boil water in it, then add the oil,lastly the noodles
  3. It's ready after 2-4 minutes. Add the spices immediately or dry the noodles if you wish

Enjoy your meal!!
