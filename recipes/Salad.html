Salad's Recipe

Choosing your favourite vegetables to make salad is avaliable. I'll give you my method of making salad. The ingredients you need:

  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • cabbages
  • Olive oil

The steps are very simple. Here's what you should do:

  1. Chop the vegetables into small pieces after washing them (my personal option. You can chop them the way you want)
  2. sink them in worm salty water for 30-60 seconds
  3. Add the olive oil, and mix them on the plate.

Enjoy your meal!!
